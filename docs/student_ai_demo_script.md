# MySmartStudy: Student AI Features Demo Script

## Overview
This script outlines the flow for demonstrating the Artificial Intelligence features available to Students in MySmartStudy across both Web and Mobile platforms. 

## Feature List (Student)
1. **AI Learning Profile & Assessment**
2. **AI Study Companion (Chat)**
3. **AI Daily Study Guide**
4. **AI Timetable Analysis**
5. **AI Exam Planner**
6. **AI Study Materials (Summaries, Flashcards, Practice Quizzes)**
7. **AI Mind Map Buddy**

---

## Presentation Script

### 1. Introduction & AI Learning Profile
**Presenter:** 
"Welcome to the student view of MySmartStudy. Our platform is deeply integrated with Google Gemini to provide a highly personalized learning experience. Everything begins with the **AI Learning Profile**."

* **Action:** Navigate to the AI Companion screen and open the Learning Style Assessment.
* **Talking Points:** 
  * "When students first interact with the app, they can take a quick AI-powered assessment quiz."
  * "Based on their answers, the AI determines their learning style (e.g., visual, auditory, reading/writing) and tracks their personalized strengths and weaknesses."
  * "This profile is constantly updated and allows the AI to tailor all its future responses and study recommendations to suit each individual student perfectly."

### 2. AI Study Planning (Timetable, Daily Guide, Exam Planner)
**Presenter:** 
"Next, let's explore how the system acts as a personal academic planner."

* **Action:** Open the **AI Timetable Analysis** feature.
* **Talking Points:**
  * "Students can simply upload a PDF of their schedule or paste their timetable text."
  * "The AI parses the schedule, identifies overlapping classes, and suggests optimized study slots right into their calendar."

* **Action:** Switch to the **AI Daily Study Guide**.
* **Talking Points:**
  * "Using data from deadlines, upcoming quizzes, and the parsed timetable, the AI generates a customized Daily Study Guide."
  * "It gives students prioritized recommendations with realistic time estimations, so they know exactly what to focus on today."

* **Action:** Show the **AI Exam Planner**.
* **Talking Points:**
  * "When finals approach, the AI Exam Planner can generate multi-day, distributed study plans that build in review periods to help prevent cramming."

### 3. AI Study Materials
**Presenter:** 
"Now, let's look at how the AI helps students actually absorb course content."

* **Action:** Navigate to Course Resources -> AI Study Materials.
* **Talking Points:**
  * "Our system uses a technique called Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). This means the AI can read the actual lecture slides, PDFs, and notes uploaded by the lecturer."
  * "Students can ask the AI to generate concise **Summaries**, interactive **Flashcards**, or **Practice Quizzes** based on specific modules."

* **Action:** Open the **AI Flashcard Viewer** (Mobile) or equivalent Web view.
* **Talking Points:**
  * "Here is the interactive flashcard viewer, completely generated dynamically from the course content to help students memorize key concepts."

### 4. AI Mind Map Buddy
**Presenter:** 
"A core feature of MySmartStudy is our collaborative Mind Map tool. But students don't have to braindstorm alone."

* **Action:** Open the **Mind Map Editor** and start a new map.
* **Talking Points:**
  * "As students build their mind maps, the **AI Mind Map Buddy** runs in the background."
  * "It continuously analyzes the structure of the map and provides intelligent recommendations—suggesting new related nodes, logical connections, and layout improvements."
  * "Students can also chat directly with the Buddy about the map's subject matter, and the AI maintains the whole memory context."

### 5. AI Study Companion (Global Chat)
**Presenter:** 
"Finally, no matter where the student is in the app, their AI Study Companion is just a tap away."

* **Action:** Tap the floating AI Companion button.
* **Talking Points:**
  * "This is more than a standard chatbot. It knows the context of the course, remembers past discussions, and knows the student's learning profile."
  * "When a student asks a complex question about a lecture, the AI retrieves the exact paragraphs from the course documents and cites its sources `[Source 1]`, ensuring the information is accurate and trustworthy."
