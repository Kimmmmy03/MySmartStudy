# MySmartStudy: Lecturer AI Features Demo Script

## Overview
This script outlines the flow for demonstrating the Artificial Intelligence features available to Lecturers in MySmartStudy. These features are designed to significantly reduce administrative burden, streamline grading, and uphold academic integrity.

## Feature List (Lecturer)
1. **AI Content Import (Course Migration)**
2. **AI Plagiarism Detection (RAG-powered)**
3. **AI Grading Assistant**
4. **AI Image Generation (Educational Graphics)**

---

## Presentation Script

### 1. Introduction
**Presenter:** 
"Welcome to the lecturer dashboard of MySmartStudy. While students get flashier study tools, our Google Gemini integration for educators is laser-focused on one main goal: saving time without compromising on quality."

### 2. AI Content Import
**Presenter:**
"The first hurdle for any lecturer is setting up their course. We've simplified this with the **AI Content Import** tool."

* **Action:** Navigate to Class Management and open the AI Import Modal.
* **Talking Points:**
  * "Lecturers can bring over content from existing syllabi or older LMS platforms easily."
  * "The AI automatically extracts text, parses the structure, and builds the course modules, assignments, and topics instantly right into the app."

### 3. AI Plagiarism Detection
**Presenter:** 
"Once a course is running, ensuring academic integrity is paramount. Let's look at the **AI Plagiarism Detection**."

* **Action:** Navigate to a Course -> Assignments -> Submissions. Select a student's submitted assignment.
* **Talking Points:**
  * "When a student submits a file, text, or mind map, the lecturer can trigger an AI-powered similarity analysis."
  * "Unlike basic checkers, our platform uses RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) coupled with Graph Analysis to cross-reference the submission against other students, past assignments, and official course materials."
  * "It generates a comprehensive Similarity Report, flagging specific sections of overlapping text and providing an overall confidence score."

### 4. AI Grading Assistant
**Presenter:** 
"Now, let's explore grading, which is traditionally the most time-consuming task for educators."

* **Action:** On the same submission screen, click the **AI Grading Assistant** button.
* **Talking Points:**
  * "The lecturer can ask the AI to recommend a grade. The system feeds the submission, the assignment instructions, and the precise Grading Rubrics into Gemini."
  * "Within seconds, the AI outputs a suggested grade, a detailed score breakdown according to the rubric, and suggested qualitative feedback to help the student improve."
  * "Most importantly: The lecturer always maintains full control. They review the AI's suggestions, edit the feedback if desired, and then apply the final grade with a single click."

### 5. AI Image Generation
**Presenter:**
"Finally, we've integrated image capabilities to help make courses more engaging."

* **Action:** (Optional) Show module creation or announcement page.
* **Talking Points:**
  * "Lecturers can use built-in AI Image Generation to dynamically create cover graphics, concept diagrams, or illustrative materials for their announcements and resources, ensuring a premium, polished feel for their classes."

### 6. Conclusion
**Presenter:**
"By automating the repetitive parts of course setup and assessment, MySmartStudy enables lecturers to spend less time on administration and more time mentoring their students."
